Speaking Head Emoji Meaning

The 🗣️ Speaking Head emoji is a pictorial representation of a person's head that has opened its mouth to speak, conveying the idea of speaking or talking. The emoji can be used to indicate that someone is about to share their thoughts, opinions, or ideas with others.
