Flag Tokelau Emoji Meaning

The 🇹🇰 emoji is a digital representation of the flag of Tokelau, an island nation located in the South Pacific Ocean. The flag features a horizontal tricolor design with blue, red, and yellow stripes, along with three white triangles above and below the middle stripe. These triangles have white borders on either side, and each triangle has a small black disk at its center, representing the three atolls of the country: Atafu, Fakaofo, and Nukunhiva.

People often use the 🇹🇰 emoji to express their connection to Tokelau or to show appreciation for the island nation's culture.
