The 🇱🇦 emoji represents the flag of Laos, a small landlocked country located in Southeast Asia. The wheel is depicted in red color against the yellow background of the flag.

People often use the 🇱🇦 emoji to represent their connection to Laos or to show support for the country's people and culture.
