The 🇸🇴 emoji represents the flag of Somalia, a country located in the Horn of Africa. The flag features a white five-pointed star on a blue background with a yellow crescent moon and an arrow pointing towards the hoist side. This design is symbolic of Somalia's rich history and cultural heritage.
