The flag of Romania has an interesting history and cultural significance that fans of the country might find fascinating. Did you know that the Romanian tricolor was introduced in 1862, during the revolution of the Principality of Romania? The colors were chosen by the national hero, Ioan Larionescu, who fought for independence from the Ottoman Empire. Today, the flag is an important symbol of Romanian identity and national pride.
