🎌 What are Kaomojis?

Kaomojis (顔文字) are popular Japanese text-based emoticons made from punctuation and symbols. Unlike standard western emojis, these represent emotions through eyes and mouth in a horizontal way. They are perfect for adding personality to your WhatsApp chats, Instagram bios, and gaming profiles.
